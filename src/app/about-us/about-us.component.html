Vision & Mission

“To produce teachers with a high purpose and intense pragmatism who will be the change agents in future.”
The specific vision and the mission enhance the long-range perspective of the College, particularly in relation to the cause of teacher education. This lends itself to the enunciation of specific goals to be achieved. The vision and the mission statements of the College focus on the two aspects, viz. the holistic approach, and the cardinal principle of equality.
