Eligibity to take admission to B.Ed. Course

  • Graduates from any Recognized University, Agricultural University, Open University, and Deemed University in; Arts, Science, Commerce, Home Science, Agriculture Science are eligible to appear for C.E.T.
  • Along with the graduates, the candidates appearing for degree examinations of March/April  from any Recognized University , Agricultural University , Open University, and Deemed University in; Arts, Science, Commerce, Home Science, Agriculture Science are eligible to appear for C.E.T.
  • Candidates belonging to open and Reserve category must secure at least 18 and 15 marks in C.E.T.
  • The candidates, willing to get admission in English Medium colleges of Education, must appear for English Language Test along with CET. It is obligatory for the candidates to secure minimum 25 marks out of 50 marks to be eligible for the admission in the same.
  • Candidates having equivalent Degrees of other universities are not eligible for B.Ed. Admission. E.g. Sanskrit  Shastri Degree, Hindi  Sahitya Sudhakar etc.
  • For applicants of Open category, Other Backward Class (OBC), and Special Backward Class (SBC) category minimum 50% in degree examination. For applicants of Scheduled Caste category minimum 45% in degree examination. If any University has semester pattern then average percentage of marks for both the semesters should be considered.
  • (Note-Attendance for the course is compulsory.Working candidates should not apply)

For the applicants that have served as a full time teacher for minimum two  years, the above mentioned minimum percentage limit is not applicable.   
      They only need to pass the qualifying examination.
B.Ed. Admission 2013 List of Required Documents

B.ED. ADMISSION 2013 LIST OF REQUIRED DOCUMENTS 

Name of Candidate:
  • Attested true copy of Statement of Marks / Passing Certificate of the Graduate Degree.
  • In case of Change in name after marriage - Attested true copy of Marriage Certificate / Government Gazette.


 Category:

  • Attested true copy of Caste Certificate / Non - Creamy Layer / Validity Certificate in your name issued by Competent Authority, clearly mentioning the Category of the Candidate and also the remarks that the Caste is under Backward Class in the State of Maharashtra. (Non-Creamy Layer should be after April-2012 )


Special Category:

If Physically Handicapped - Attested true copy of minimum 40% Physical Disability Certificate and eligible to complete the B.Ed.course issued by Competent Authority / District Civil Surgeon.

  • If Kashmir Migrant - Attested true copy of the Certificate clearly mentioning "Displaced Jammu and Kashmir Candidate staying in refugee camp" from head of the officer of Migrant/Refugee camp.
  • If Ward or Spouse of Ex-Serviceman or Active-Serviceman - Attested true copy of valid Certificate issued by Competent Authority.
